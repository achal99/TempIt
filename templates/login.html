What is TempIT?

Students

TempIT connects part-time workers with companies to make it easy for students to find work that fits with their busy schedules. Simply sign up as a student, then find work at a time that suits you!

Companies

TempIT makes it simple for businesses to find part-time workers. It's as easy as signing up and posting shifts and part-time positions for students to take up!
